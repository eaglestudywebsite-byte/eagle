About Eagle Study Loan

Empowering dreams across borders with premium education financing solutions

Our Story

Learn about our journey and commitment to helping students achieve their global education dreams

Pioneers in Education Financing

Founded in 2010, Eagle Study Loan has emerged as a trusted partner for Indian students aspiring to pursue higher education abroad. Our journey began with a simple vision: to make global education accessible to deserving students regardless of their financial background.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of students achieve their dreams of studying at prestigious universities across the globe. Our commitment to transparency, flexibility, and student-centric solutions has made us one of the most preferred education loan providers in India.

We understand that every student's journey is unique, which is why we offer personalized loan solutions tailored to individual needs and circumstances.

Our Approach

At Eagle Study Loan, we believe that financial constraints should never be a barrier to quality education. Our approach is built on three core principles:

  • Accessibility: Simplifying the loan process to make it accessible to students from diverse backgrounds
  • Transparency: Maintaining complete transparency in our processes, terms, and conditions
  • Support: Providing end-to-end support throughout the loan journey and beyond

Our dedicated team of education loan experts works closely with each student to understand their specific needs and craft the perfect financial solution for their academic journey.
